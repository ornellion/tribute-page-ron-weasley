The Harry Potter Lexicon Timeline

The major events that shaped the life of Ron Weasley:

  • 1st March 1980: Ron Weasley is born the youngest son of Molly and Arthur Weasley
  • 1983: Ron develops a deathly fear of spiders when his brother Fred turns his teddy bear into a spider because Ron broke his toy broomstick.
  • 1st September 1991: Ron meets Harry Potter on the Hogwarts Express on the way to their first journey to Hogwarts. Both sorted into Gryffindor, thus begins their adventure.
  • May 1992: Ron sacrifices himself in a life-sized chess game so that Harry is able to go on to save the Stone.
  • 1st September 1991: Ron and Harry fly his father’s Ford Anglia to Hogwarts.
  • July 1993: Ron and his family visit their Bill Weasley in Egypt where his brother is working as a curse-breaker.
  • August 1994: Ron alongside his siblings and best friend join the Minister for Magic and the Bulgarian delegation in the Top Box to watch the Irish win against Bulgaria in the Quidditch World Cup. Chaos ensues in the campground that night culminating in the first sighting of the Dark Mark in years
  • August 1995: Ron becomes a Gryffindor Prefect alongside Hermione Granger.
  • September 1995: Ron becomes the Keeper for the Gryffindor Christmas Team. He goes to prove that Weasley is indeed our King.
  • July 1996: Ron receives his OWL results. He Exceeds Expectations in a number of subjects including Defence Against the Dark Arts, Potions and Transfiguration.
  • 1997 - 1998: Ron is thought to have spattergoit for the duration. It is later learned that he accompanied the Man Who Lived in his fight against the Dark Lord Voldemort.
  • September 2000: Ron marries his lifelong best friend Hermione Granger. He is rumoured to have gotten very drunk at the ceremony.
  • January 2001: After revolutionising the Department of Magical Law Enforcement as an Auror, Ron leaves to run the Weasley’s Wizard Wheezes alongside his brother George.
  • 6th January 2006: Ron and Hermione welcome their daughter Rose Granger-Weasley into the world.
  • 7th May 2008: Their son Hugo Granger-Weasley is welcomed into the world.
